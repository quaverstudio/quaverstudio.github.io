# Transform Your Audio

Quaver lets you adjust pitch and tempo effortlessly, with studio-quality results on your mobile or desktop device.

[Download on Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=org.taosha.quaver){: .btn} 

## Key Features

### Change Pitch
Modify the pitch of any audio file without affecting its duration. Perfect for musicians practicing in different keys.

### Tempo Control
Speed up or slow down playback precisely. Master difficult passages by practicing slowly.

### Studio Quality Export
Render your changes to high-quality audio files. (Available in Quaver Studio)
